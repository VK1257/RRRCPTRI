Bachelor of Physiotherapy

Get ready to make a difference in people’s lives through the power of physiotherapy. Welcome to our Bachelor of Physiotherapy Program. At GCU, our Bachelor of Physiotherapy program covers a wide range of key courses that form the foundation of your knowledge. You will delve into basic science subjects such as Anatomy and Physiology, as well as pre-clinical subjects like Pathology and Microbiology. In the clinical domain, you will explore areas such as Neurology, Orthopaedics, and various physiotherapy subjects like Biomechanics, Exercise Therapy, and Electrotherapy.
What sets GCU apart is our focus on practical, hands-on learning. We understand the importance of real-world experience, which is why we provide extensive exposure to electrotherapeutic and exercise therapeutic devices. You'll have the opportunity to work with specialized tools and techniques like Mobilization and Manual Therapy, enabling you to develop the necessary skills to excel in the field of physiotherapy.
Research-based learning is highly encouraged at GCU. We believe in nurturing a culture of inquiry and innovation. We support our students in conducting clinical research and even assist them in filing patents. With our emphasis on research, you’ll have the chance to contribute to advancements in the field of physiotherapy.
GCU holds accreditations and collaborations with professional associations, further validating the quality of our program. We have established partnerships with Multispeciality hospitals and Special schools, providing you with valuable industry exposure and practical insights into the profession.
To ensure that our curriculum remains industry-driven, we regularly invite guest lecturers and industry speakers who share their expertise and experiences. This exposure helps you stay updated with the latest advancements and trends in the field of physiotherapy.
Entrepreneurship opportunities and mentorship programs are integral parts of the GCU experience. We believe in nurturing your entrepreneurial spirit and providing you with the necessary guidance to succeed. Our mentorship programs connect you with experienced professionals who can offer valuable advice and support throughout your academic journey.
Additionally, GCU offers professional certifications in specialized areas such as Myofascial Release, Dry Needling, Advanced Neurological Techniques, Sports Physiotherapy Techniques, Winter Sports Management Techniques with special focus on Advanced Pain Management and ICU Techniques. These certifications enhance your skills and broaden your career prospects.
Students get to experience hands-on clinical practice at Physiocare, our on-campus Physiotherapy outpatient center. We prioritize student involvement, providing a valuable opportunity for practical learning.
At GCU, we value cross-disciplinary collaboration. We provide opportunities for you to collaborate with students and faculty from different disciplines, fostering a holistic approach to learning and problem-solving.
We have international university tie-ups in Europe specializing in Physiotherapy, offering you the chance to participate in study abroad programs. This global exposure expands your horizons and provides a multicultural learning experience.
In terms of specialization, GCU offers a range of options, including Advanced Neurological Techniques, Sports Physiotherapy Techniques, Paediatric Techniques, Manual Therapy, and ICU Techniques. These specializations allow you to focus on areas that align with your interests and career goals.
GCU also recognizes the importance of co-curricular activities and offers an Agility Club where you can engage in various activities that promote physical fitness and well-being.
Furthermore, we believe in fostering linguistic diversity, which is why we offer foreign language classes in French, German, and Spanish. This additional skill can open doors to global opportunities and facilitate communication with diverse patient populations.
At GCU, we are committed to providing you with the best learning experience in the field of physiotherapy. Our industry collaborations, practical focus, research opportunities, international ties, and comprehensive curriculum make us the ideal choice for pursuing a Bachelor of Physiotherapy in Bangalore.
Join us at Garden City University and embark on an exciting journey toward becoming a skilled and compassionate physiotherapist. Apply now and unlock your potential with us!
